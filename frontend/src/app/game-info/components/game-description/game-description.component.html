Rules:

  1. There are two factions playing the game: Humans and Zombies.
  2. The job of a human is to remain human. The goal of a zombie is to convert as many humans as possible.
  3. To convert a human, a zombie has to tag them. To tag someone means to touch them.
  4. Once converted, a player is a zombie for the rest of the game.
  5. In order to stop a zombie from tagging them, a human can shoot them with a Nerf gun or throw a clean, rolled up sock at them. This stuns the zombie
  6. A stunned zombie has to stand still for 10 seconds.
